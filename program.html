Food Security

Ensuring no one in our community goes hungry

Our food security program provides comprehensive nutrition support to individuals and families facing food insecurity. We operate multiple food pantries, hot meal services, and nutrition education programs.

Services Offered

📌 Weekly food pantry distribution

📌 Hot meal programs at community centers 7 days a week

📌 Mobile food delivery for homebound individuals

📌 Nutrition education and cooking classes

📌 Fresh produce from community gardens

Housing Support

Providing safe and stable housing for those in need

Our housing program offers emergency shelter, transitional housing, and support services to help individuals and families achieve housing stability and independence.

Services Offered

📌 Emergency shelter with 24/7 access

📌 Transitional housing programs

📌 Rental assistance and housing navigation

📌 Case management and support services

📌 Housing search and placement assistance

Education & Skills

Empowering through education and job training

We provide educational opportunities and job training programs that help individuals develop skills, gain employment, and achieve economic self-sufficiency.

Services Offered

📌 GED preparation and testing

📌 Computer literacy and digital skills

📌 Job training and certification programs

📌 Resume writing and interview preparation

📌 Job placement and career counseling

Mental Health & Wellness

Supporting mental health and overall well-being

Our mental health program provides accessible counseling, support groups, and wellness services to help individuals overcome trauma, manage stress, and build resilience.

Services Offered

📌 Individual counseling sessions

📌 Support groups and peer counseling

📌 Crisis intervention services

📌 Substance abuse support programs

📌 Wellness activities and stress management

Children & Family Services

Supporting families and nurturing children

We provide comprehensive support for families with children, including childcare assistance, after-school programs, and family strengthening services.

Services Offered

📌 After-school tutoring and enrichment

📌 Summer camp and recreational programs

📌 Parenting classes and family counseling

📌 Childcare assistance and referrals

📌 School supplies and clothing distribution

Employment & Job Training

Empowering through education and job training

Our employment services program helps job seekers develop skills, connect with employers, and achieve economic stability through sustainable employment.

Services Offered

📌 Job readiness training

📌 Career counseling and job placement

📌 Professional clothing and interview supplies

📌 Resume writing and interview preparation

📌 On-the-job training and apprenticeships

A Holistic Approach to Support

We understand that challenges are often interconnected. That's why our programs are designed to work together, providing comprehensive support that addresses multiple needs simultaneously.

Each individual receives personalized case management to ensure they have access to all the resources and support they need to achieve stability and independence.

Help Us Expand Our Impact

Your support allows us to reach more individuals and families in need, expand our programs, and create lasting change in our community. Together, we can build a future where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.
